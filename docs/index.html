Privacy Policy for Tap Championship

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Tap Championship respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, how it is used, and what the app does not collect.

Summary

  • Tap Championship does not use GPS or collect your precise location.
  • The country you choose in the game is a gameplay choice only. You may choose any available country, and it does not need to match where you live.
  • The app does not show in-app ads.
  • Donations are handled outside the app through an external website.

Information We Collect

Tap Championship is designed to use as little personal information as possible. The app may store:

  • A username you choose
  • Your password for account login
  • A system-generated login identifier derived from your username for authentication purposes
  • Basic game progress and profile data, such as your selected country, total taps, pending taps, coins, cosmetic items, avatar background color, and account timestamps

Information We Do Not Collect

  • GPS or precise location data
  • Your real-world country of residence unless you choose to reveal it yourself in your username or other content
  • In-app payment information
  • Advertising identifiers for in-game ads

How Information Is Used

  • Create and manage your account
  • Let you sign in and keep your session active on your device
  • Save your game progress and cosmetic selections
  • Show gameplay-related features such as rankings, stats, and country selection

Donations

If you choose to donate, you are sent to an external donation page. Donations are not processed inside Tap Championship. Any information you provide on that external site is governed by that third party's own privacy policy and terms.

Third-Party Services

Tap Championship uses third-party services for backend and authentication support. These services may process the limited account and gameplay data needed for the app to function.

  • Supabase, for authentication, session handling, and app data storage

If you visit an external donation page, that external provider may also process your information under its own policies.

Data Storage

Some data is stored locally on your device, such as your login session, so you can remain signed in. Account and gameplay data are also stored in the app backend so your progress can be saved.

Children's Privacy

Tap Championship is not intended to knowingly collect more personal information from children than described in this policy. If you believe personal information has been provided inappropriately, please contact the app owner to request review or deletion.

Your Choices

  • What username to use
  • Which country to select in the game
  • Whether to use the donation link
  • Whether to continue using the app

Because usernames are chosen by players, please avoid using your real name or other personal information in your username if you want to remain more private.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When changes are made, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised.
